What is Netwroking?
Computer networking is the practice of connecting multiple computing devices (such as computers, servers, routers, and switches) to facilitate communication and resource sharing. A computer network allows devices to exchange data, share hardware like printers, and access the internet, creating a vast interconnected system that powers everything from local area networks (LANs) to the global internet.

Why Networks?
computer networks enable resource sharing, communication, collaboration, and access to remote services, making them fundamental to modern computing.

How old is networking?
Computer networking, in its modern form, began to take shape in the 1960s. computer networking is about 55 years old, with its roots dating back to the 1960s, evolving significantly since then to become an integral part of daily life.
